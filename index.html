Scope of services

// A single point of accountability, from lease negotiation through project closeout.

SVC-01

Lease Negotiations

Support the leasing team through negotiation, customize work letters to project specifics, produce feasibility test-fits, and introduce design criteria and MEP requirements to prospective tenants.

SVC-02

Design Management

Track design and permit milestones in conformance with the lease, review tenant drawings for compliance and aesthetic alignment, and verify MEP drawings against base-building capacity.

SVC-03

Construction Management

On-site management of tenant construction to monitor progress, with review of the construction budget, GC qualifications, and schedule.

SVC-04

Budgeting

Establish landlord work budgets — soft and hard costs — from historical data and contractor input, review and process TI submissions, and procure and manage consultant contracts.

SVC-05

Retail Scope of Work

Maintain the retail lease tracker and prepare weekly reports for client review, keeping ownership informed at every milestone.
