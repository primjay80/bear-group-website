Scope of services

// A single point of accountability, from lease negotiation through project closeout.

SVC-01

Lease Negotiations

Support the leasing team through negotiation, customize work letters to project specifics, produce feasibility test-fits, and introduce design criteria and MEP requirements to prospective tenants.

SVC-02

Design Management

Track design and permit milestones in conformance with the lease, review tenant drawings for compliance and aesthetic alignment, and verify MEP drawings against base-building capacity.

SVC-03

Construction Management

On-site management of tenant construction to monitor progress, with review of the construction budget, GC qualifications, and schedule.

SVC-04

Budgeting

Establish landlord work budgets — soft and hard costs — from historical data and contractor input, review and process TI submissions, and procure and manage consultant contracts.

SVC-05

Retail Scope of Work

Maintain the retail lease tracker and prepare weekly reports for client review, keeping ownership informed at every milestone.

Principal & practice

// PJ Bautista, Principal

Twenty-plus years delivering complex, high-value retail projects across the Washington, D.C. metropolitan area.

PJ Bautista founded Bear Group in 2026, bringing with him a distinguished career in retail design and construction. Most recently, he served as Director of Retail Design & Construction at JBG Smith, where he was the primary point of contact for a portfolio of high-profile retail projects — overseeing the full tenant lifecycle from base-building design and lease negotiation through construction and closeout.

Throughout his career, PJ has earned a reputation for delivering projects on time and in alignment with ownership objectives, building lasting trust with stakeholders and tenants alike. His deep-rooted relationships with permitting agencies, general contractors, architects, and consultants are the cornerstone of how Bear Group operates today.
